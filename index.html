Is this a medical service?

No. AI WELLNESSCORE is an educational tool designed to provide insights into your lifestyle and general well-being. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified health provider for any medical concerns.

How is my data protected?

We take your privacy seriously. Your quiz answers are used solely to generate your report. Your photo is processed in real-time and is deleted immediately after analysis. We do not store or share your personal data. All payments for premium reports are handled securely by Stripe.

Do I need to upload a photo?

The photo analysis is a key part of our holistic approach, providing insights into non-obvious wellness indicators. While you can skip this step, we highly recommend it for the most accurate and comprehensive report.
