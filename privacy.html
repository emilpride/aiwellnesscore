AI WELLNESSCORE Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025

Digital Global Solutions LLP ("we", "us", or "our") operates the AI WELLNESSCORE service. This policy explains how we collect, protect, and use your information so you can feel confident engaging with our experience.

1. Information we collect

We only gather data needed to build your personalized insights:

  • Quiz responses: Lifestyle, health, and wellness answers you provide.
  • Contact details: Your name and email so we can deliver your report.
  • Facial photograph: A single selfie uploaded for AI analysis.
  • Technical diagnostics: IP address, browser, and device type to keep the experience running smoothly.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe—we never store card numbers.

2. How we use your information

Your data powers the AI WELLNESSCORE experience:

  • Create your tailored wellness report and insights.
  • Process secure payments for premium upgrades.
  • Send summaries, updates, or essential account notices.
  • Improve accuracy and performance of our AI systems.

3. Data retention & deletion

  • Photograph data: Your selfie is transmitted to our analysis provider, processed instantly, and permanently deleted right after the assessment.
  • Quiz data: Answers and report outputs are stored under an anonymous session ID and automatically purged after 90 days.

4. Trusted third-party services

We collaborate with secure, industry-leading partners:

  • Stripe for payment processing.
  • Netlify for hosting and serverless infrastructure.
  • Google Firestore for encrypted session storage.
  • Face++ & Google Gemini for AI-driven facial and data analysis.

We never sell or trade your personal information. Access is limited strictly to delivering the service.

5. Security

All transmissions occur over SSL, and data is safeguarded in accordance with industry standards. Internal controls ensure sensitive information remains protected.

6. Policy updates

We may revise this policy to reflect improvements or regulatory requirements. Updates appear on this page with a refreshed effective date.
